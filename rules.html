TrekCraft Server Rules

Guidelines for maintaining a safe, inclusive, and productive community

Quick Navigation

General Server Rules

These rules apply to all members of the TrekCraft community, whether you're a visitor, builder, or staff member. Our goal is to maintain a safe, inclusive environment where everyone can enjoy building and collaborating together.

Zero Tolerance Policies
  • No Harassment: Any form of harassment, bullying, or targeted abuse is strictly prohibited
  • No Hate Speech: Bigotry, racism, sexism, homophobia, transphobia, anti-semitism, or any form of discrimination is not tolerated
  • No Exploitation: Do not exploit server vulnerabilities, glitches, or technical issues
  • No Griefing: Do not intentionally damage, destroy, or interfere with others' builds
Community Standards
  • Respect Others: Treat all community members with respect and dignity
  • Constructive Communication: Disagreements should be handled maturely and constructively
  • Follow Staff Guidance: When staff members ask you to move channels or discontinue conversations, please comply
  • No Spam: Avoid excessive messaging, repetitive content, or disruptive behavior
  • Appropriate Content: Keep content suitable for our community (no explicit NSFW content in general channels)
Technical Rules
  • No Cheating: Do not use unauthorized mods, hacks, or exploits
  • Respect Server Resources: Avoid actions that could cause server lag or performance issues
  • No Bypassing: Do not use private messages or mail systems to bypass server rules
  • Permission Required: Do not modify others' work without explicit permission

Discord Community Rules

Our Discord server is the heart of our community. These rules help maintain a positive, inclusive environment where everyone can connect, collaborate, and share their passion for building.

Channel Guidelines
  • Stay On Topic: Keep discussions relevant to the channel's purpose
  • Use Appropriate Channels: Post content in the correct channels (builds in #builds, questions in #questions, etc.)
  • Respect Channel Topics: Follow staff guidance when asked to move discussions
  • Spoilers: Post spoilers only in designated #spoilers channels
Content Guidelines
  • No AI-Generated Art: Avoid posting AI-generated art, music, or video content
  • NSFW Content: Keep adult rated content in NSFW marked channels only
  • Respectful Discussions: Political discussions should be respectful and constructive
  • No Spam: Avoid excessive messaging, repetitive content, or disruptive behavior
Community Interaction
  • Respectful Debates: Disagreements should be handled maturely and constructively
  • No Drama: Interpersonal issues should be settled privately or with staff help
Voice Chat Guidelines
  • Respect Others: Be mindful of background noise and volume levels
  • No Recording: Do not record voice chats without explicit permission
  • Appropriate Language: Keep language suitable for our community except in NSFW channels. Hate Speech, racism, sexism, etc is never tolerated
  • Join/Leave Gracefully: Be respectful when joining or leaving voice channels

Builder Rules & Standards

As a TrekCraft builder, you're part of a collaborative team working on ambitious, long-term projects. These rules ensure quality, consistency, and a positive building environment.

Building Standards
  • Planning Required: Always use planning blocks for any project before building
  • Scale Standards: Corridors generally should be 3-4 blocks wide, decks 3-5 blocks tall
  • Hull Work First: Complete hull-cleaning and deck planning before interior work
  • Fill Dead Space: Minimize exposed block faces and empty areas
  • Modern References: Try to use current, modern builds as 1:1 reference. Inspiration from older projects is fine
Project Management
  • Group Contribution: Every builder is encouraged to support community builds, or group builds when possible
  • Staff Approval: Staff approval is not required for new projects, but we do appretiate a heads up on new imports
  • Archive Policy: Map backups are limited. Builders are responsible for archiving and backing up their own work
Quality & Feedback
  • Open to Feedback: Be receptive to both positive and constructive criticism
  • High Standards: We hold each other to S-tier building standards
  • Constructive Criticism: Provide helpful, specific feedback to other builders
  • Continuous Improvement: Focus on learning and growing, not defending past work
  • Plot Limits: Each builder has their own 2500x2500 block plot. Please keep any projects within the bounds of these plots. Staff can assign group yards when needed
Technical Limitations
  • Lag-Heavy Blocks: Limit use of Item Frames, Custom Models, Doors, Redstone, Beds
  • Signage: Limit to hero areas or planning purposes only
  • Performance: Consider server performance when building large structures
  • Resource Usage: Be mindful of server resources and storage

Recruit & Trial Builder Rules

Recruits and trial builders are in a learning phase, working to demonstrate their skills and fit within our community. These rules help ensure a smooth transition into full builder status.

Application Process
  • Solo Projects Only: Applications must be individual work, not group collaborations
  • Age Requirement: Applicants must be 16+ years old
  • Resource Usage: Use our modpack and custom blocks in your application

Regardless of the outcome of your application, we ask that all applicants remain respectful of our team

Trial Period Expectations
  • Communication: Maintain regular communication with your liaison and staff
  • Feedback Receptiveness: Be open to feedback and willing to iterate on your work
  • Timeline Adherence: Joining our team is joining for the long term. Please be patient during your Trial period, there are no points for finishing first
  • Community Engagement: Participate in team activities and discussions
  • Learning Focus: Demonstrate willingness to learn and improve
Project Guidelines
  • Smaller Scope: Start with manageable projects to learn our standards, don't bite off more than you can chew!
  • Mentor Guidance: Work closely with your mentors, and your liason. They're here to help you succeed
  • Group Participation: Contribute to existing group projects
  • Skill Development: Focus on learning our building techniques and standards
  • No Solo Projects: Avoid starting personal projects during trial period
Reapplication Process
  • Application Rejection: Can reapply after 3 months with improved work
  • Trial Failure: Can reapply after 6 months if issues are addressed
Important Notes

Enforcement: These rules are enforced by our staff team at their discretion. Violations may result in warnings, temporary restrictions, or removal from the community.

Updates: Rules may be updated as our community evolves.

Questions: If you have questions about any rules or need clarification, please ask staff members.

Appeals: If you believe a rule enforcement was unfair, you may appeal to the staff team.
