About Our Program

TrekCraft is the longest-running sci-fi megabuilding community in Minecraft. We create massive, detailed ships inspired by Star Trek and other sci-fi universes, with builds that take months or even years to complete.

Our Builder Program offers a structured path for talented builders to join our collaborative projects. You'll start with focused trial builds, receive guidance from a dedicated liaison, and gradually take on larger projects as you develop and get to know our team.

Who We're Looking For

We want more than just skilled builders â€” we're looking for teammates. Joining the TrekCraft build team means becoming part of a casual, collaborative crew that values creativity, communication, and community.

Constructive, Mature, and Open-Minded

  • You're open to feedback and see it as part of the creative process
  • Our review style is direct, honest, and always focused on helping the team grow
  • You treat others with respect and are comfortable in a group of mostly young adult builders
  • You're mature enough to handle disagreements with grace and humor

Self-Sufficient & Team-Oriented

  • You can work independently, without needing constant check-ins
  • You understand this is a team-first environment. We build together. We succeed together
  • You're excited to contribute ideas, share WIPs, and push the project forward

Passionate About the Project

  • You're in it for the long haul. Many of our builds take months or even years to complete
  • You're enthusiastic about Star Trek, TrekCraft, and the lore-inspired design work we do
  • You enjoy sharing your work with the community â€” on Discord, in screenshots, on socials

Social & Casual

  • While there's no minimum activity requirement, we value folks who get involved when they can
  • That could mean hopping in VC during build sessions, joining our movie nights, or just chatting in Discord
  • We're not strict. We're nerds building nerdy things for fun

Application Timeline

We review your application build and responses within 1-2 weeks using our evaluation rubric. We're looking for technical skill, creative vision, and alignment with our team values. If your application shows promise, you'll receive the Recruit role in Discord, and access to our Recruit channels.

Recruits in the trial phase will not be able to start solo-projects until their promotion to full builder. We want to encourage new members on the team to work collaboratively to avoid burnout, and to have more opportunity to work with different members of our build team.

Note: Both application review and trial completion are evaluated using our standardized rubric to ensure fair and consistent assessment.

Your Application

Ready to apply? Here's everything you need to know about submitting your application:

We want to see your best effort, regardless of experience level. Don't worry about being perfect â€” we're looking for passion, creativity, and a solid attempt that shows you understand our requirements.

Note: Your application must be a solo project. We do not accept group applications, and any application that appears to have been assisted by another builder will be declined.

Application Plot System

All applicants will use our official application world/blueprint containing 9 themed plots, each 50x50 blocks. You must build a minimum of 6 plots, and you can choose any combination of the 9 available themes.

Build Requirements & Guidelines:
  • Slice of Life: Each plot should feel like a complete slice of a full build. For example, a "Living" plot might include a corridor with entries to quarters, a family suite, and a tailor â€” showing variety within the theme.
  • Modern Design: Use modern layout practices (hallways 3â€“5 blocks wide, decks 3â€“4 blocks of air between them) and our custom blocks (not just vanilla)
  • Space Usage: Plots don't need to be "full" (using every inch of block space), but should make good use of the area to showcase your abilities
  • No Height Limit: There is no vertical height limit, as long as the entire plot can be captured in a few pictures
  • Photos Required: Submit clear photos/screenshots of your finished plots as part of your application

Important!

All applicants must build using our current 1.21.1 modpack.

Available Plot Themes

We've picked out 9 themes that are essential to any project. You'll pick at least 6 of these themes to represent on your application. While the themes are fixed, your interpretation is not. Feel free to ignore our example room ideas and come up with whatever you think fits best.

Recreation

Holodeck simulations, lounges, arboretums, sports facilities, or entertainment areas.

Engineering

Main Engineering, Jefferies Tubes, EPS conduits, power distribution, or systems.

Tactical

Armories, security offices, training simulators, or defensive systems.

Science

Labs, research bays, astrometrics, exobiology, or experimental facilities.

Medical

Sickbay, medical labs, quarantine wards, or treatment facilities.

Living

Crew quarters, officer suites, mess halls, or communal living spaces.

Operations

Cargo bays, transporter rooms, computer cores, or logistical facilities.

"Hero" Area

Large, dramatic locations like bridges, shuttlebays, or stellar cartography.

Wildcard

No theme restriction â€” let your creativity run wild!

Preparation & Resources

Take advantage of all available resources to create your best application:

  • Review our evaluation rubric to understand what we're looking for
  • Explore our current builds on the server for ideas and reference
  • Use our marker blocks to help you layout ideas and plan
  • Join our Discord to ask questions and chat with our team
  • Study our resource pack and modded blocks to understand the tools available
  • Take your time and put in the effort â€” quality over speed

Age Consideration

To maintain a comfortable space for all of our builders (mostly in their late teens to 30s), we limit applicants to age 16+. Maturity, respect, and communication skills are non-negotiable regardless of age.

What If I'm Declined or Don't Pass Trial?

We understand that being declined can be disappointing, but it's not the end of your TrekCraft journey. Here's what you should know:

Application Decline

If your initial application isn't accepted, you'll receive specific feedback about areas for improvement. Common reasons include:

  • Plots or application is incomplete, or missing key elements
  • Technical skills need development (block usage, layout planning, etc.)
  • Application responses don't align with our team values
  • Age requirement not met

You can reapply after 3 months with an improved build and application. Focus on addressing the specific feedback you received, and consider spending more time studying our existing builds and resource pack before reapplying.

Trial Period Challenges

If you don't complete the trial period successfully, it's usually due to:

  • Inconsistent communication or absent for majority of trial
  • Difficulty adapting to our collaborative workflow
  • Lack of on server work to evaluate
  • Team dynamics or maturity concerns

You can reapply after 6 months if you feel you've addressed the feedback and are ready to try again. Use this time to practice building, improve your communication skills, and demonstrate your commitment to collaborative work.

Staying Connected

Even if you're not ready to join the build team, you're welcome to:

  • Join our Discord community as a visitor
  • Participate in community events and discussions
  • Share your builds in our art & related channel
  • Apply again when you feel ready

Our goal is to build a strong, collaborative team. Sometimes that means waiting for the right fit or giving applicants time to grow. We also know that this entire process may seem daunting, or even silly. We get it, and we appretiate the time and effort that every application displays - Thank you.
