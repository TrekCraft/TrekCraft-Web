TrekCraft Build Team Applications Are Now Open!

JULY 14th, 2025

Build Team Applications Are Open

We're excited to announce that TrekCraft build team applications are now open!

TrekCraft is the longest-running sci-fi megabuilding community in Minecraft. We build massive Star Trek ships — some take months or even years to complete. It's a collaborative environment where we work together on these huge projects.

What We Do

We build massive ships (500+ meters) with dozens of decks, using our custom resource pack and suite of mods. Our builds respect canon and push what's possible in Minecraft. It's not just building — it's being part of a team that cares about the work.

The Process

Here's how it works:

  • Application: Build 6+ themed plots using our 1.21.1 modpack
  • Trial Period: Roughly 4-8 weeks working with the team
  • Full Builder: Access to everything and your own projects
What We Look For

We want teammates, not just builders. You need to be:

  • 16+ with good communication skills
  • Able to work independently but also collaboratively
  • Committed to long-term projects
  • Passionate about Star Trek and sci-fi building
  • Open to feedback and willing to learn
  • Social and casual — we're nerds building nerdy things
The Reality

This isn't easy. Our builds are complex, our standards are high, and it takes time to learn our style. But if you're willing to put in the work, learn from feedback, and be part of a team, it's incredibly rewarding.

We're honest about the effort required because we want people who are ready for the challenge. But we also know that the best builders often start with passion rather than perfect skills.

Ready to Try?

If you're interested, start by reading our complete application guide. It has all the details about what to build, how to apply, and what to expect.

Application Guide Visit TrekCraft Join Discord

Even if you're not ready to join the build team, you're welcome in our Discord community. Many of our current builders started as visitors.

We appreciate everyone who takes the time to apply. It shows you care about what we do, and that's what matters most.

Live long and prosper! 🖖

Questions about the application process? Join our Discord or check out our complete application guide.
