1.21.1 Mod List

Below is a full list of what mods are included in this pack. These mods are downloaded automatically by our launcher using the official CurseForge & Modrinth API's. However, users can download each mod individually from their respective sources if they prefer. TrekCraft & Astral Mod's are downloaded from our internal storage hosting.

Please Note

We only provide support for our Prism modpack. Downloading mods individually for use with other launchers is not supported. You'll be on your own if something doesn't work, or if these links become outdated. We also do not provide instructions for how to manually setup the TrekCraft pack.
